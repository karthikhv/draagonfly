- The Product

A hidden design, revealed by cold.

Dragonfly cups are coated with thermochromic pigments that react when a chilled drink is poured in. As the surface temperature drops, hidden artwork appears in full clarity, then fades as the cup warms. This creates a repeatable reveal moment that turns every sip into a branded interaction.
