— Who We Are

Crafting moments brands never forget.

Dragonfly Manufacturing Ltd. is a Hong Kong based team focused on one thing: making cold activated cups that feel sharp, useful, and memorable. We work directly with brands, agencies, retailers, and event teams that want the product to land well, not just look good on paper.

From print setup to packing, we keep the work under one roof so the details stay consistent and the process stays straightforward. Our facility in Sheung Wan handles both careful finishing and high speed production runs — without compromising on quality.
