— Who We Are

Crafting moments brands never forget.

Dragonfly Manufacturing Ltd. is a Hong Kong-based manufacturer specializing in custom cold-activated color-changing cups. We work directly with brands, agencies, retailers, and event companies worldwide to deliver products that create genuine moments of surprise and delight.

From concept to finished product — we handle printing, production, and packaging under one roof. Our team in Sheung Wan, Hong Kong brings together precision manufacturing and creative flexibility, so your brand gets exactly what it needs.

Whether you need 500 cups for a product launch or 50,000 for a global campaign — we scale with you.
